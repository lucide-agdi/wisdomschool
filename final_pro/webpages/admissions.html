Student Registration

Welcome to WISDOM SCHOOL Rubavu branch! We are excited to have you join our learning community. The registration process is simple, and this page will guide you through the necessary steps to enroll your child in our school.

Registration Process

To register your child, please fill out the form below with accurate information. Ensure that you have all required documents, such as proof of residence and previous school records, ready for submission. After submitting the form, our administrative team will review your application and contact you with further instructions.

Important Information

Please note that registration is open for new students from 3/10/2024 to 15/10/2024. We encourage you to complete your registration early to secure your child's place in our programs. If you have any questions about the registration process, feel free to reach out to our office at Contact information.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for admission, students must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be of appropriate age for the grade level.
  • Must provide a birth certificate or other legal proof of age.
  • Must have previous school records if applicable.
